What is Monster Hunter Rise?

Monster Hunter Rise is a game developed by Capcom, and released on Nintendo Switch in March 2021, with a PC version being released in January 2022. In the game, you play as a hunter, hunting monsters that are causing problems for people, whether that be destroying trade caravans, causing trouble with farming crops, or attempting to cause a world ending disaster.

To hunt these monsters, you are given a choice of 14 different weapons to choose from, varying from a small sword and shield or a lance with a tower shield, to a large axe that can morph into a sword. Each of the 14 weapons in the game have very differnt movesets that compliment different playstyles, with there being ranged weapons for those who don't want to get too close, small and fast weapons who want to be able to get in and hit the monster while also having time to evade the monsters attacks, and slow and heavy weapons that take time to power up their attacks, but when they hit, they hit extremely hard.

In the game, you also have access to buddies who can help you in combat. You have the choice between a palico, a cat-like creature able to support you in combat by applying various status effects to either you or the monster, or a palamute, a dog-like creature who can help you to quickly traverse the map.

Weapons



Greatsword

The greatsword is a slow, but high damaging weapon, relying on timing your charged attacks to hit the monster, while also not being hit yourself and knocked back out of your attack.

Longsword

The Longsword is a fast weapon, relying on various counter moves, to dodge the monsters attacks with invulnerability, and strike back.

Sword & Shield

The sword and shield is a weapon containing a small sword and shield. You use the sword to slash at the monster, and while you can use the shield to guard, its main purpose is to bash the monster with.

Dual Blades

The dual blades are all about speed. You use your speed to get in and hit the monster during small openings, while also having combos you can perform during larger openings.

Lance

The lance shows that the best offense is a good defense. with the massive shield you have, you are able to guard against many attacks and stick next to the monster, allowing you to attack and punish openings by sticking close.

Gunlance

The gunlance shares the defensive capabilities of the lance, while also having the ability to shoot the monster with your shells.

Hammer

The hammer is all about bashing the monsters head in. When hitting the head with the hammer, it will build up a stun meter, eventually leading to the monster being knocked down for a few seconds.

Hunting Horn

The hunting horn is similar to the hammer, being a blunt weapon. However, it differs by allowing you to use your attacks to play songs that apply buffs to you and your teammates.

Switch Axe

The switch axe is a weapon that can either function as a sword or an axe, depending on the situation. The weapon relies on switching between forms allowing combos in and out of both modes.

Charge Blade

The charge blade is another morphing weapon, allowing you to switch between a large sword and shield and a massive axe. The weapons relies on gaining energy in sword mode, and unleashing it with massive attacks in axe mode.

Insect Glaive

The insect glaive allows you to pole vault and jump above the monster while attacking. It also grants you control of a kinsect, an insect that helps by buffing you.

Bow

The bow is a fast weapon, allowing you to dodge and fire rapidly. It allows you to get in close while being fast enough to dodge attacks.

Light Bowgun

The light bowgun is a small ranged weapon that can use many different types of ammo to shoot at the monster. Its small size allows it to be very mobile on the battlefield.

Heavy Bowgun

The heavy bowgun is similar to the light bowgun, having a variety of ammo types at its disposal, but differs in size. The larger size means it has very low mobility, but it makes up for it by having much greater damage output.

Monsters



Magnamalo

Magnamalo is a monster clad in purple armor. On its forelegs and tail, it weilds blades to attack the hunter with. It also utilizez hellfire, a purple cloud of flame that will, after some time explode, damaging any hunter caught in it

Crimson Glow Valstrax

Crimson Glow Valstrax is a variant of the monster Valstrax. They resemble a jet, having an air intake on their chest, and using their wings as boosters to quickly propel itself.

Pukei Pukei

Pukei Pukei is a monster that utilizes its poisonous tongue to attack hunters and inflict them with poison. It is also able to use its tail for poison.

Bazelgeuse

Bazelguese is a monster that utilizes its blasting scales to blow up hunters. Bazelguese likes to defent its territory, flying into interrupt any conflicts between a hunter and another monster.

Monster Hunter Rise Sunbreak

Monster Hunter Rise Sunbreak is an expansion releasing on June 30, 2022. This expansion will add more monsters, more moves for each weapon, and an overall higher difficulty for content taking place in this expansion
